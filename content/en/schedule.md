## 💻 Course Registration System Overview

Course registration takes place at **[sugang.handong.edu](https://sugang.handong.edu)**. You'll log in using your HISNet ID and password. If the system feels unfamiliar at first, don't worry—the preliminary registration period gives you plenty of time to practice and get comfortable with it before the main registration begins.

---

## 🔑 Understanding Preliminary vs. Main Registration

This is a crucial distinction to understand clearly.

### 🛒 Preliminary Registration = Shopping Cart

Think of preliminary registration as filling up a **shopping cart at an online store**. You're pre-selecting the courses you want to take, but nothing is finalized yet.

Here's what's important: **adding courses to your cart doesn't guarantee enrollment.** The outcome depends on one thing:

- **Number of applicants ≤ Course capacity**: Everyone who added courses during preliminary registration will have their registrations **automatically confirmed**. No further action needed.
- **Number of applicants > Course capacity**: If more students applied than there are seats, **nobody's registrations are confirmed**, not even those in the preliminary round. Everyone must reapply during main registration.

Therefore, after preliminary registration closes, you **must log into HISNet and check which of your courses were confirmed**. If any courses weren't confirmed, you'll need to be ready to apply again quickly during main registration.

### ⚔️ Main Registration = The Battle for Seats

Main registration is literally a **battle for limited seats**. Popular courses that filled up during preliminary registration will close instantly when main registration opens. Success depends on being ready at exactly 10:00 AM and applying as fast as possible.

| 2/23 (Mon) | Entrance Ceremony | The official day you become a Handong student. |
| 2/23 (Mon) – 2/27 (Fri) | HanST (Freshman Orientation) | **EPT (English Placement Test)** will be administered during this period. Your English course assignment depends on this score, so attendance is absolutely mandatory. |
| **2/26 (Thu) 10:00–16:00** | **Preliminary Registration** | This is your "shopping cart" phase. Add the courses you want and use this time to become comfortable with the system. **After it ends, you must check HISNet to confirm whether your courses were accepted.** |

| 2/28 (Sat) 14:00 | Dormitory Move-in | Residence hall check-in. |
