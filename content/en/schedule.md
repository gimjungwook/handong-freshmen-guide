## 💻 Course Registration System Overview

Course registration takes place at **[sugang.handong.edu](https://sugang.handong.edu)**. You'll log in using your HISNet ID and password. If the system feels unfamiliar at first, don't worry — the preliminary registration period gives you plenty of time to practice and get comfortable before the real action begins.

---

## 🔑 Understanding Preliminary vs. Main Registration

This is a crucial distinction, so let's break it down clearly.

### 🛒 Preliminary Registration = Shopping Cart

Think of preliminary registration as filling up a **shopping cart at an online store**. You're pre-selecting the courses you want, but nothing's finalized yet.

Here's the important part — and we really need you to get this: **adding courses to your cart doesn't guarantee enrollment.** The outcome depends on one thing:

- **Number of applicants ≤ Course capacity**: Everyone who added the course gets **automatically confirmed**. You're in! No further action needed.
- **Number of applicants > Course capacity**: If more students applied than there are seats, **nobody gets confirmed** — not even those who applied during preliminary registration. Everyone has to compete again during main registration.

So after preliminary registration closes, you **must log into HISNet and check which courses were actually confirmed**. If any weren't confirmed, you'll need to be ready to apply again quickly during main registration. Don't skip this step!

### ⚔️ Main Registration = The Battle for Seats

Main registration is literally a **battle for limited seats**. Popular courses that were oversubscribed during preliminary registration will close within seconds once main registration opens. Success depends on being ready at exactly 10:00 AM and moving as fast as you can. It's intense, but with preparation, you've got this!
